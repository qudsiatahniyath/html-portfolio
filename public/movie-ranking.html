My top 3 movies

1. 3 idiots

I enjoy this movie a lot as it is based on an engineer's life which is also my profession. It has tons of life lessons, filled with good humour One should watch it if they want to have a good laugh and warm vibes.

2. M.S Dhoni

This is a Biopic of a famous cricketer- M.S. Dhoni. The movie depicts his struggling days, journey as a cricketer and his personal life. It is a must watch for Dhoni admirers.

3.Tiger

Tiger is a movie having Mr. Salman Khan as it's lead. The movie is packed with action scenes with a thrilling story. Mr. Salman's movies never fail to amaze his admirers and are appreciated all over the globe
